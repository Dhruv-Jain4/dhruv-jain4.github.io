Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026  ·  Developer: dhruv@Makeyoursoftware (Dhruv Jain)

✦  The short version: MindDump is built to respect your mind. Your thoughts, tasks, and ideas are stored only on your own device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal content with anyone. Ever.

1. Who We Are

MindDump is a mobile application developed by Makeyoursoftware (Dhruv Jain). The app is available on the Google Play Store. You may contact us at djamitjn@gmail.com.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

MindDump is an offline-first application. The following data is stored exclusively on your device using a local on-device database (Hive) and is never transmitted to any server operated by us:

  • Your thoughts, worries, and ideas ("dumps")
  • Your tasks and checklists
  • Your reflection responses and resolved statuses
  • Your usage streaks and mental clarity scores
  • Your app preferences and settings (including theme and app lock status)

We do not create user accounts. We do not require you to provide your name, email address, or any personal identifiers to use MindDump. We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or any usage telemetry from your device.

3. Third-Party Services (Advertising & Purchases)

MindDump integrates with the following third-party services to provide certain features. Each has its own privacy policy which governs their data practices.

Google AdMob (Advertising)

Free users of MindDump see advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID), and location data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this app or other websites.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings or your device's privacy settings.
  • For more information on how Google uses your data, please visit: Google’s Privacy & Terms.

RevenueCat & Google Play (In-App Purchases)

MindDump offers in-app purchases (e.g., Lifetime Pro). All payments are processed securely through the Google Play Store billing system. We do not directly collect, process, or store your credit card information or banking details.

  • We use RevenueCat to manage and validate these purchases. RevenueCat assigns an anonymous App User ID to track your purchase history and unlock premium features.
  • RevenueCat's Privacy Policy can be found at: revenuecat.com/privacy.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

All data you create in MindDump is stored locally on your device using the Hive local database. If you uninstall the application, this data is permanently deleted from your device. We have no ability to recover deleted data, as no copy exists on our servers.

The optional Export / Import feature allows you to create a backup file (.json) that is saved directly to your device's storage. This file is controlled entirely by you.

5. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data. Since all of your personal content is stored exclusively on your own device and never transmitted to our servers, we do not maintain servers that hold your personal data.

Your device's built-in security (Android encryption, screen lock) protects your locally stored data. The optional App Lock feature adds an additional biometric layer of protection at the app level. We encourage you to keep your device software up to date to benefit from the latest security improvements.

6. Automatic Data Deletion

MindDump is designed to forget on purpose. Tasks and checklists are automatically deleted at midnight each day. Worry thoughts set for reflection are automatically processed. This deletion occurs entirely on your device and is a core, intentional feature of the application.

7. Biometric Data

The optional App Lock feature uses your device's native biometric authentication (fingerprint or face recognition). MindDump never receives, stores, or transmits any biometric data. Authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system (Android).

8. Push Notifications

MindDump may send you local push notifications to remind you of scheduled reflection times. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. We do not use a cloud notification service and do not track whether you open notifications.

9. Children's Privacy

MindDump is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, there is no data held by us to access, export, or delete.

To permanently delete all your data from the app, use the "Burn it down" option in Settings → Data & Privacy. You may also simply uninstall the application, which removes all locally stored data from your device.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of MindDump after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
